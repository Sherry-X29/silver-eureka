About Us

At Pizzarro, great pizza starts with real passion.
Founded with a simple idea - that good food brings people together - Pizzarro has grown into a place where flavour, comfort, and tradition meet. Each pizza is carefully prepared by hand using dough made fresh daily and ingredients selected for their taste and quality. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or sharing a meal with friends, we're here to make it memorable - one slice at a time.
